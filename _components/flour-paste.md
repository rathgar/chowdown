tags: [baking,decoration]
prepTime: PT3M

ingredients:
  - 2 tbs flour
  - cold water

directions:
  - Take a small quantity of flour and add just enough water to form a thick paste.
  - Spoon into a small bag and cut off a little corner.
  - Pipe onto your buns before they go in the oven.
