ingredients:
  - 2 cups kale rinsed
  - 1½ cups fresh pineapple
  - 1 tbsp chia seeds
  - 1 ripe banana peeled
  - 1½ cups unsweetened almond milk
  - Maple syrup / honey - optional
  - 1½ cups ice

directions:
  - Place all ingredients in a blender. Blend until fully mixed and smooth.
  - Serve immediately.
